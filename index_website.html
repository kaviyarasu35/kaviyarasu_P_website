Our Story

Built by people who were tired of logistics being the excuse.

Kavi Private Limited started with a simple complaint: too many businesses were told "it's stuck in transit" and left to just wait.

We began as a small fleet running regional routes for manufacturers who couldn't get a straight answer from bigger carriers. We answered the phone. We tracked the load ourselves. Word got around, and the fleet grew.

Today we move freight, run warehouses, and handle last-mile delivery for businesses across the region — but we kept the part that made us different: a dispatch desk that picks up, a rate that doesn't change after pickup, and a team that treats your delivery deadline as our own.

We're not the biggest logistics company you'll talk to this week. We're the one that calls you before you have to call us.

Direct communication

A named coordinator for every account — not a ticket number in a queue.

Transparent pricing

Quoted rates hold. No surprise surcharges once your freight is loaded.

Owned fleet, owned accountability

Most of our vehicles are ours, so there's no third party to blame when something needs fixing.
